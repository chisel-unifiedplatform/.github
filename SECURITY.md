# Security Policy

The Orphix team takes the security of our platform and our users' data seriously. We
appreciate the work of security researchers in keeping Orphix safe.

## Reporting a vulnerability

**Please do not report security vulnerabilities through public GitHub issues, pull
requests, or discussions.**

Instead, report them privately using one of these channels:

1. **Preferred:** GitHub's [private vulnerability reporting](https://docs.github.com/code-security/security-advisories/guidance-on-reporting-and-writing-information-about-vulnerabilities/privately-reporting-a-security-vulnerability)
   — open the **Security** tab of the affected repository and click **Report a vulnerability**.
2. **Email:** [security@orphix.ai](mailto:security@orphix.ai)

Please include, as much as you can:

- The type of issue (e.g. injection, auth bypass, data exposure).
- The affected component, repository, or URL.
- Steps to reproduce or a proof of concept.
- The potential impact.

## What to expect

- **Acknowledgement** within **3 business days**.
- A **triage assessment** and severity rating shortly after.
- Regular updates on remediation progress.
- Credit for your responsible disclosure once a fix is released, if you'd like it.

## Scope

This policy covers Orphix-owned services and the repositories in this organization.
Please act in good faith: only test against your own accounts/data, avoid privacy
violations and service degradation, and never exfiltrate data.

Thank you for helping keep Orphix and our users safe.
